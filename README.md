#  Bright Motors Car Sales Analysis (BRIGHTLEARN)

Comprehensive data analytics project designed to extract business insights for the newly appointed Head of Sales at Bright Motors. This analysis uses historical car sales data to uncover performance trends, revenue drivers, and strategic opportunities.


Project Purpose

The goal of this project is to support strategic decision-making at Bright Motors by analyzing its historical car sales dataset.  
The insights help the Head of Sales with:

- Expanding dealership presence  
- Improving sales performance  
- Identifying revenue-driving vehicle segments  
- Understanding customer buying behavior  

Objectives

✔ Identify top-performing vehicle makes & models  
✔ Analyze pricing, mileage, and year-of-manufacture relationships  
✔ Determine regions with the highest sales volumes  
✔ Uncover emerging customer purchasing trends  
✔ Provide data-driven recommendations for profitability  

Tools & Technologies

1. Data Cleaning - SQL, Excel    
2. Data Visualization - Power BI, Excel Charts    
3. Analytics - DAX, Statistical Analysis    
4. Documentation - Markdown, PDF Reports      


Key Insights

- Black, White, Gray, and Silver vehicles generate the highest revenue  
- Red and Blue perform moderately, while rare colors show minimal demand  
- Automatic transmissions dominate (~97%) across every color and segment  
- Popular brands/models consistently outperform in both unit sales and revenue  
- Clear regional disparities identified—some states significantly outperform others  
- Strong link found between year, condition, and selling price

Business Recommendations

- Prioritize automatic vehicles in neutral colors for inventory  
- Expand dealership capacity in high-performing States 
- Optimize pricing strategies using condition-year-price trends  
- Minimize low-demand color and manual-transmission combinations  
- Use historical trends to forecast upcoming high-demand segments
